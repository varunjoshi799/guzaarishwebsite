Who We Are

16 members | Unlimited memories

We are Georgetown University's premiere Bollywood fusion dance team. Founded by 4 friends in 2019, we train in multiple styles including hip-hop, bhangra, raas, contemporary and many more. The team performs routines at school performances and last year we competed in two competitions in Chicago, IL and Santa Barbara, CA

We're is a close knit family that has an incredibly fun time at each practice and focuses on each member's growth as dancers and as individuals. We are super excited to grow our family over the net year and continue to compete all over the country!

Culture

We use dance to express and appreciate our South Asian heritage

Growth

Built of dancers from all different backgrounds, together we create a talented, cohesive family

Community

We're more than a dance team, we're a family who supports and cares for each other. No matter what.

Interested In Joining?

We are always looking for members to join our family. We have workshops and auditions in the beginning of fall and spring semesters and would love to see you there! If you're interested in joining, fill out the interest form below and let us know if you have any questions or concerns!
