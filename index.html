Who We Are

16 members | Unlimited memories

We are Georgetown University's premiere Bollywood fusion dance team. Founded by 4 friends in 2019, we train in multiple styles including hip-hop, bhangra, raas, contemporary and many more. The team performs routines at school performances and last year we competed in two competitions in Chicago, IL and Santa Barbara, CA

We are a close knit family that has an incredibly fun time at each practice and focus on each member's growth as dancers and as individuals. We are super excited to grow our family over the next year as continue to compete all over the country!

Culture

We use dance to express and appreciate our South Asian heritage

Growth

Built by dancers from all different backgrounds, together we create a talented, cohesive family

Community

We're more than a dance team, we're a family who supports and cares for each other. No matter what.

Interested In Joining?

    FAQs

    Everything you need to know, with a twist (headphones recommended)

    What are practices like?

    We have three 3 hour long practices a week. We focus on perfecting our set for competitions and work hard to perform our best. If your muscles don't ache from the runs, your stomach surely will from all the laughing - practices are as much for bonding with the team as they are for learning the dances!

    What is competition season like?

    It's the most fun you'll ever have! Imagine lifelong memories across the US, dancing on stages and getting cheered by hundreds in the audience, all while being surrounded by amazing members of the South Asian community. You will spend an endless amount of time with the team, not only through practices but also traveling and just hanging out - be warned though, you won't get much sleep!

    How does it feel to be part of Guzaarish?

    A groupchat that never sleeps. A squad at every party. A family that always supports you. Dancing till the dead of night. Oh, but you'll have to get used to the jealously of all your non-guzaarish friends though.

    What's the best part of Guzaarish?

    Not so much a singlar best part, more like 16 best parts that we just can't decide between. Good news is we do have space for some more best parts. Ask us the question next year though ... we might just say you.
